Class 10 Mathematics Revision Notes

1. Real Numbers
- Review Euclid's division lemma.
- Practice HCF and LCM based questions.

2. Polynomials
- Learn zeroes of a polynomial.
- Solve graph-based questions for understanding.

3. Linear Equations
- Revise substitution and elimination methods.
- Practice word problems daily.

4. Quadratic Equations
- Memorize the quadratic formula.
- Solve at least five equations every revision session.

5. Trigonometry
- Learn standard angle values.
- Revise identities and application-based questions.
